Maintaining a trading journal is essential for long-term success in the markets.
It allows you to document every trade, helping you analyze your decisions objectively.
By recording your strategies, emotions, and outcomes, you can identify patterns, strengths, and weaknesses.

A journal serves as a roadmap for continuous improvement, helping you refine your approach, avoid repeated
mistakes, and build discipline.
It also helps track your progress over time, offering valuable insights into your growth as a trader. Ultimately,
a well-kept journal is your personal guide to developing consistency and better decision-making in trading.
